Privacy Policy

QuietLog · Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mahmut Gazihan Arslan ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the QuietLog mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data generated by the App remains on your device.

  • Microphone audio: The App accesses your microphone in real time to compute sound level (dB) values. Raw audio is never recorded or stored — only the calculated decibel value is used.
  • Session data: Noise sessions, decibel samples, and alert events are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Apple Health: If you grant permission, the App may write hearing-related data to Apple Health. This data is governed by Apple's Health app privacy settings and Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Purchase information: In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

3. Microphone Permission

The App requires microphone access to measure sound levels. This access is used solely for real-time dB computation on-device. You may revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → QuietLog → Microphone. Revoking permission will disable the core measurement feature of the App.

4. Apple Health Integration

With your explicit permission, QuietLog can write noise exposure data to Apple Health. This data is stored within Apple's HealthKit framework on your device and is subject to Apple's privacy practices. We never read health data from HealthKit.

5. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the App may send local notifications to alert you about high noise levels. These notifications are generated on-device and no notification data is transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All App data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by deleting the App. Deleting the App permanently removes all locally stored session data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted to this page. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact
